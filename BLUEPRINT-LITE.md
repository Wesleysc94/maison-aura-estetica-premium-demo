# Maison Aura Estetica - Blueprint Lite

## Positioning

Premium aesthetic website built to communicate delicacy, authority, and a refined sense of care in the beauty and med spa space.

## Core Direction

- light premium composition with soft contrast;
- elegant typography and calm rhythm;
- beauty branding with stronger editorial direction;
- structure designed for trust, aspiration, and lead capture.

## Main Sections

1. Hero
2. About / positioning
3. Treatments
4. Social proof
5. Specialist credibility
6. Contact
7. Footer

## Interaction Logic

- the opening should feel aspirational and polished;
- treatment sections must communicate clarity and value;
- the brand should feel premium without becoming cold;
- the structure should remain flexible for multiple beauty sub-niches.

## Design Tokens

- Base background: soft ivory or warm neutral
- Main text: charcoal
- Accent: champagne, sand, rose, or muted premium tone
- Support text: warm gray

## Why This Works

- raises perceived value in a highly visual niche;
- supports both emotional branding and conversion;
- adapts well to clinics, med spas, and expert personal brands.
